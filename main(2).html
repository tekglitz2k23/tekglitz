TEKGLITZ

About Jerusalem

Jerusalem College of Engineering was established in the year 1995 under the umbrella of Jerusalem Education Trust to impart quality engineering education. Over the period of 23 years it has made a mark of itself and got established as an institution which offers excellent engineering education. The Institute has very good infrastructural facilities and laboratories with state-of-the art equipment. The institute is accredited by NAAC and NBA.

About TekGlitz

"Tek Glitzz" is an exciting symposium scheduled for October 14th, 2023. This event promises a blend of technical and non-technical activities, making it an inclusive opportunity for students from diverse academic backgrounds to participate. If you have a passion for technology or simply enjoy a fun and enriching atmosphere, this symposium is the perfect platform for showcasing your talents and engaging with a vibrant community of like-minded individuals. Get ready for a day of learning, innovation, and entertainment at "Tek Glitzz"!

Description

exhilarating debugging adventure as we dive deep into the world of software troubleshooting! Our Debugging Frenzy event is designed to sharpen your debugging skills, enhance your problem-solving abilities, and boost your confidence when tackling those tricky coding issues. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, this event has something for everyone. Whether you're looking to fine-tune your skills or learn from the best, Debugging Frenzy has it all. Register now and get ready to conquer those bugs like a pro. See you there!

Rules

 The competition will consist of two rounds.  The time duration for Round 1 is 20 minutes with 3 Questions including C, Java, and Python language will be asked.  The time duration for Round 2 is 30 minutes has 1 Question, that can be chosen by participants can either be C or Java.  Your submissions after the given time will NOT be considered.  The Judge's decision is final. Any MALPRACTICES will not be tolerated and if found, the participants will be DISQUALIFIED! Participants are NOT allowed to browse the internet. If found doing so, the participant will be DISQUALIFIED! Participants are not allowed to discuss any aspects of the problem with other participants during the challenge.
